Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for the bot's functionality, including Discord User IDs, Server IDs, and interaction logs for the purpose of rate limiting and service improvement.

2. Data Usage

Logs are used to provide AI responses and ensure compliance with our terms. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Retention

Interaction logs are stored in "hot" storage for 30 days, after which they are archived and purged from our active database.

4. Security

User-provided API keys (BYOK) are encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256-CBC encryption.

5. Your Rights

You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us through our support server.
